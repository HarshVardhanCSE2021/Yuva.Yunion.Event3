WHO ARE WE?
We are the Yuva Yunion, a community made for the youth by the youth.
We are the age of today by which we mean we are the adult teenagers who are sitting idle and looking for something to work towards to have a sense of achievement through these tough times.
This can also be termed as work away from work, for people who want to do something productive other than studying.
We have departments for people interested in different creative areas such as content and creation, research, technological work made with people who reek of talent, only here to provide you with the best of everything that we are going to be covering on our Instagram page and other social media platforms too.
